Mindfulness is all about being present in the moment. Each time that you purposely bring your focus back to the present, you train your brain and mindfulness skills.

A common meditation to train mindfulness is to focus on your breathing. Focal Point helps you focus on your breath by engaging multiple senses to reduce distraction.

Press your mouse or tap your screen as you inhale. Let go during your exhale. Keep your focus on the present: your breath and the sights and sounds that accompany it.
